<objective>
Close **D1** and **D2** (medium) — the two Telegram reaction defects — in one plan (same component stack).

- **D1 (REACTION_INVALID):** most emoji reactions on Telegram fail with HTTP 400 `REACTION_INVALID` from tapi `message/reaction`. Root cause: the quick-bar offers `{👍 ❤️ 😂 😮 😢 🙏}` and the "+" picker offers the full ~300-emoji `ReactionEmojiCatalog`, but Telegram only accepts its standard free reaction set (👍 👎 ❤️ 🔥 🥰 👏 😁 …) unless the chat enabled custom reactions. `😂` and `😮` (and most catalog emoji) are NOT in that set. Fix: constrain the reaction bar + picker to the Telegram-allowed set WHEN `ActiveChannel == Telegram` (WhatsApp byte-identical), and make a 400 revert cleanly anyway (the optimistic pill already reverts in `PostReactionRoutine`; the chat-list preview does NOT — fix that too).
- **D2 (removal never clears):** removing an own reaction succeeds server-side (gone in the Telegram app) but never clears in-app — worse than the accepted one-cycle flicker, it never self-heals. Root cause: `TelegramReactionMerge.Merge` preserves a FRESH optimistic "me" entry (`IsFreshOptimistic`, 90s grace) with no concept of an optimistic REMOVAL, so a just-added-then-removed reaction is re-added on every poll for the grace window. Fix: a removal tombstone/suppression in the reconcile that distinguishes optimistically-ADDED from optimistically-REMOVED.

Purpose: reactions the owner can pick actually stick on Telegram, and a removal stays removed — while WhatsApp is untouched.

Constraint: WhatsApp reaction UX byte-identical. No server/n8n changes.
</objective>
