**3. Success moment (ONB-03)**
- On successful auth (either channel), «Бот подключён!» shows with the green check animating (DOScale 0.9→1 OutBack) — it does NOT auto-dismiss in 2s.

- For a "both channels" creation, the success moment appears ONCE (not twice), after the bot exists.
- Settings re-auth of a bot that ALREADY has uploaded files: the primary CTA reads «Открыть чаты» instead.

**4. Checklist (ONB-04)**
- BotsPage shows «Первые шаги» with «N из 4» + progress bar; rows appear with a 0.05s cascade.
- Row 2 label matches the bot's actual channel («Подключить WhatsApp» or «…Telegram»).
- Each row deep-links: create→AddBotPanel, connect→bot settings, upload→«Прайс-листы», first-reply→Chats (row-4 tap shows the hint «Попросите знакомого написать вам…»).
- Completed rows strike through; after uploading a price list + receiving a first outgoing reply, at 4/4 the card hides and stays hidden on relaunch.

**5. Zero regression (ONB-05)**
- Empty state, AddBotPanel auto-open (post-onboarding, flag set), and both auth flows behave as before.
- EditMode suite green (record the count).
